The Best Movies According to Karolis

My Top 3 Movies of All Time

Inception (2010)

A thrilling sci-fi film where dreams and reality collide. Leonardo DiCaprio leads a team to plant an idea in someone's mind.

Gladiator (2000)

An epic tale of revenge and honor. A Roman general, betrayed and enslaved, fights for justice in the Colosseum.

The Godfather (1972)

A legendary mafia story about power, loyalty, and family. Michael Corleone rises to lead his family's empire.
